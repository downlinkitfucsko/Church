Worship at Hampton Park

Gathering together in music, prayer and reflection every Sunday

Sunday Services

Our community gathers in worship each Sunday at 10:00 am at 1 Coral Drive, Hampton Park. Whether you are a long-time member or visiting for the first time, you will find a warm and genuine welcome.

Services run for approximately one hour and include congregational singing, prayer, and a reflection on Bible passages that help us understand who Jesus is and what it means to follow him today.

Children in Worship

We warmly encourage children to be part of our Sunday worship. We believe that children belong in the heart of the church community. Age-appropriate activities are available, and families are welcome to sit together throughout the service.

Online Worship

Can't be with us in person? We record our Sunday services and make them available online. You can watch recent services and catch up on past messages.

New to Church?

If you are new to church, or returning after some time away, you are very warmly welcome. Our congregation is a diverse, friendly group of people of all ages and backgrounds. There is no dress code and no expectation. Simply come along and experience community.

If you have questions before visiting, please don't hesitate to contact us — we'd love to hear from you.
