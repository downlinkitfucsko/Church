Worship at Hampton Park

Our community gathers in worship each Sunday at 10:00 am. Services include music, prayer, and a reflection on Bible passages to help us understand who Jesus is for us today. We warmly encourage children to participate.

Community Support Service

Our faith inspires us to support those going through difficult times. Our dedicated team offers free, strictly confidential assistance. Visiting services include PCLC Legal Service, Monash Health, and Orange Sky.

Arts at Uniting Place

We offer an art space where local artists and community groups display their creative works. The Hampton Park Artist Group meets the first Saturday of each month, 11am–1pm, with workshops by guest artists.
