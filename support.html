Community Support Service

Free, confidential support for people going through difficult times

About Our Support Service

Our Community Support Service is driven by our faith conviction that every person deserves care, dignity, and practical help in times of need. Our dedicated, trained team is here to listen and to connect people with the services and resources they need.

All assistance is free of charge and strictly confidential. We do not judge, and we do not require any religious affiliation to receive support.

Confidential support is available every Thursday.
Simply come in and ask at reception. No appointment is necessary.

How We Can Help

Our team can assist with a wide range of needs, including:

  • Emergency food relief and grocery vouchers
  • Utility bill assistance and financial hardship referrals
  • Housing support and homelessness referrals
  • Mental health and wellbeing support referrals
  • Domestic violence support referrals
  • Connection to government services and Centrelink assistance
  • General information, advocacy, and pastoral care

If we cannot help directly, we will connect you with the right service that can.

Emergency Relief

In addition to our regular Community Pantry, we are able to provide emergency food hampers and basic supplies for individuals and families in crisis. Please speak with a member of our support team on any Thursday, or contact us by phone or email.

Get in Touch

If you or someone you know needs support, please do not hesitate to reach out. You are not alone, and we are here to help.
