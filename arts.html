Arts at Uniting Place

Celebrating creativity and expression in our community

Our Art Space

Hampton Park Uniting Place is proud to offer a dedicated art space for local artists and community groups to display and create their work. We believe that creativity is a gift to be shared, and that art has the power to connect people, build community, and enrich lives.

Our gallery wall is regularly refreshed with new works from community members and local artists. If you are an artist or group interested in displaying your work, please get in touch.

Hampton Park Artist Group

The Hampton Park Artist Group meets on the first Saturday of each month, 11:00 am – 1:00 pm, at 1 Coral Drive, Hampton Park. The group welcomes artists of all skill levels and backgrounds.

Each session features a workshop or demonstration by a guest artist, followed by open studio time and conversation. It is a warm, supportive environment for anyone who loves to create.

Annual Art Exhibition

Each year, Hampton Park Uniting Place hosts its Annual Art Exhibition, celebrating the creative talents of our local community. The exhibition features works by community members, local artists, and program participants across a range of media including painting, drawing, photography, printmaking, and craft.

Next Exhibition: Saturday 14 March 2026

Entry to the exhibition is free and all are welcome. Works are also available for sale, with proceeds supporting our community programs.
