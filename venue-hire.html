Our Facilities

Hampton Park Uniting Place offers a range of well-maintained, affordable spaces available for hire by community groups, organisations, and individuals. Our facilities are centrally located in Hampton Park and are suitable for a wide variety of events and activities.

As a community-focused organisation, we are particularly pleased to support local groups, not-for-profit organisations, and events that benefit the Hampton Park community.

Our beautiful garden provides a unique outdoor setting for appropriate events and gatherings.

What's Included

  • Tables and chairs
  • Audio visual equipment (where available)
  • Kitchen access (on request)
  • Toilets and accessible facilities
  • Ample on-site parking
  • Disability access

Hire Rates

Hire rates are available on application and vary depending on the space required, duration, and nature of the event. We offer concession rates for not-for-profit organisations and community groups.

Please contact us to discuss your requirements and receive a quote.

Conditions of Hire

All hirers are required to agree to our standard conditions of hire, which include responsible use of the facilities, respect for neighbours, and compliance with all relevant laws and regulations. Full details will be provided upon application.

Please note that the sale or consumption of alcohol on the premises requires prior written approval.

Make an Enquiry

To check availability or make a booking enquiry, please contact us by phone, email, or using our online contact form. We aim to respond to all enquiries within two business days.
