Privacy Policy

This church is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information we collect

We may collect your name and email address if you contact us through this website. We also use basic web tools (like cookies) provided by our host, Netlify, to see how many people visit our site.

2. How we use it

We only use your contact information to respond to your specific inquiries. We do not sell or share your data with outside marketing companies.

3. Third-Party Services

Our site may show videos from YouTube or process donations through third-party providers. These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

4. Security

We take reasonable steps to ensure your information is secure, though no internet transmission is 100% secure.
